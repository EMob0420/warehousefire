SoCal Warehouse Fire Explorer

654 structure fires across SoCal, 2005–2024

This tool maps structure fires reported to FEMA's National Fire Incident Reporting System (NFIRS) across Los Angeles, San Bernardino, Riverside, and Orange Counties from 2005–2024. Use the filters to explore fires by county, year, building size, or cause. Marker size reflects property loss; color reflects structure type or cause depending on the selected mode.

Fires are categorized as Warehouse fires (properties classified as PUC 891 or buildings ≥10,000 sq ft) or Building fires (all other commercial and industrial structures). While FEMA does not separately track warehouse fires, buildings over 10,000 sq ft in these counties are reasonably assumed to include warehouse and distribution center use.
